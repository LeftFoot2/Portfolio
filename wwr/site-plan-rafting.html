Overview

Purpose

This sight is to help you plan an exciting journey with your family or as individuals. We offer a wide range of options of water rafting experiences.

Audience

We cater to the young and the more experienced riders! We have a wide variety of rapids that will appel the the most daring travelers and those that want a calmer ride.

Q: What level of difficulty do you have?

A: We offer class 1-5 rafting experiences. For Class 4 and 5 you must show that you have esperance with rafting.

Q: How much would it cost to bring my family?

A: The cost will range from for different number of people. Bring multiple people gives a discount over single costumers. Price also ranges for the age of the costumer and the time you are planning on being on the waters. Look at our comprehensive page for prices for your situation.

Q: Do I have to bring my own gear?

A: Gear will be provided for all the costumers. It is suggested that you bring water and food in water prof bags in backpacks, if you plan on being out for a long period of time.
